## PAGE 3 — STUDIO THESIS

**H1 (Cormorant Italic, 96px):**
> The studio where aesthetics solve for performance.

Most studios optimise for either image or performance. Mirrou is built as a creative system that aligns editorial aesthetics, performance logic and operational control in one architecture. ## PAGE 4 — DUAL-CITY ARCHITECTURE

Mirrou operates from two cities because the work lives in two modes: deep craft and outward movement. Hamburg anchors the studio in production discipline and structural calm. Berlin adds cultural velocity, experimentation and network density. Together, they form a single creative system — Hamburg as the reliable base layer, Berlin as the exploratory interface.

**Hamburg — Headquarters**
Base of operations and legal HQ. Primary studio for production, asset orchestration and system implementation. Environment optimised for long-focus work, documentation, quality control and delivery.

**Berlin — Second Studio Presence**
Concept development, collaboration and cultural sensing. Access to a dense network of creatives, galleries, events and partners. Ideal for early-stage ideation, prototyping of visual worlds and live collaboration.

## PAGE 5 — POSITIONING

Mirrou Creative Studio is a Hamburg-based performance creative studio with a second studio presence in Berlin. ## PAGE 13 — VISUAL LANGUAGE

Mirrou's photography is organised in three repeating registers:

**Technical Macro**
High-contrast lighting, focus on textures, surfaces and precision components. Cool color temperature.

**Editorial Workspace**
Candid, moody captures of creative process. Hands, screens, the intersection of human and machine.

**Abstract Data**
Cinematic visualisations of metrics. Glowing elements in dark environments. The Algorithm side of the brand.

**Color grading directive:** lifted blacks, desaturated midtones, emphasis on blues and warm gold highlights, grain overlay at 5%.
