## What this kit contains

This package is the official Mirrou Creative Studio press photo kit. It contains ~60 curated high-resolution images organised across five categories — Mirrou key visuals, project case visuals, studio environment, abstract and editorial concept — together with credits, captions and editorial usage rules.

The kit is intended for editorial press, curators, partners and academic reference. For commercial or campaign re-use, contact `rights@mirrou.studio`.

All photography by Olha Yevtushenko / Mirrou Creative Studio unless explicitly stated otherwise in `CREDITS_AND_RIGHTS.csv`.

## How to use this kit

1. Open `CREDITS_AND_RIGHTS.csv` and confirm the usage policy for any file you intend to publish.
2. Use `CAPTIONS.csv` to copy the short or long caption for each file.
3. Apply credits exactly as written:
   - Photographs: `Photo: Olha Yevtushenko / Mirrou Creative Studio`
   - Logos / brandmarks: `© Olha Yevtushenko / Mirrou Creative Studio`
4. Do not crop, recolour or composite without written approval from `rights@mirrou.studio`.
5. Always link to `mirrou.studio` when publishing online.

## Important notice — editorial concept frames

A small subset of frames inside `05_EDITORIAL-CONCEPT/` carries names other than Mirrou's documented cases (e.g. Aether, Neuroflow, Mininium). These frames are explicitly **editorial concept frames produced by Mirrou Creative Studio** to demonstrate the studio's visual range. They are **not** real products or third-party brands.

When using these frames, always include the caption:
> "Editorial concept frame — not a real product or third-party brand."
