**Effective:** 2026-05-09
**Owner:** Mirrou Creative Studio
**Contact:** rights@mirrou.studio

---

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to every image, document and asset distributed inside the Mirrou Press Photo Kit, the Mirrou Studio Mission Deck, the Case Study Compilation 2026 and the Personal Brand Portrait Kit, including all derivative caption and credits files.

By downloading any of these assets, the recipient accepts the terms below.

---

## 2. Permitted Uses

The following uses are permitted without further written approval, provided the credit line in section 4 is applied:

- Editorial press coverage in print, digital and broadcast media.
- Curatorial reference for exhibitions, catalogues and academic writing.
- Internal partner reference (decks, briefs, internal newsletters).
- Industry awards submissions where Mirrou is credited as the studio and Olha Yevtushenko as the photographer.
- Social media coverage that links to `mirrou.studio` or tags @olha_yevtushenko / Mirrou Creative Studio directly.

---

## 3. Prohibited Uses

The following uses are **never** permitted without explicit written approval from `rights@mirrou.studio`:

- Commercial advertising, paid social, or sponsored content by third parties.
- Use as part of another brand's identity, marketing material, or product packaging.
- Re-sale, re-licensing, or distribution of the assets.
- AI training datasets, model fine-tuning, or any machine-learning ingestion.
- Generation of synthetic imagery in the style of Mirrou or Olha Yevtushenko.
- Cropping, recolouring, retouching, mirroring, or compositing the assets.
- Use without credit, or with a credit that misattributes the work.
- Use that implies false endorsement of a person, brand or political position.
- Use in contexts that are defamatory, discriminatory or unlawful.

---

## 4. Credits

The default credit line is:

> Photo: Olha Yevtushenko / Mirrou Creative Studio

Logo and brandmark assets carry:

> © Olha Yevtushenko / Mirrou Creative Studio

For visuals where a different photographer is named in `CREDITS_AND_RIGHTS.csv`, that photographer must be named first, followed by Mirrou:

> Photo: {External Photographer} / Mirrou Creative Studio

When publishing online, please link `Mirrou Creative Studio` to `mirrou.studio`.

---

## 5. Modifications

No modifications are permitted without written approval. This includes:

- Cropping beyond standard editorial layout requirements.
- Recolouring, including converting to black-and-white unless a black-and-white version is provided in the kit.
- Adding text, graphics, watermarks or filters to the image.
- Combining the asset with other imagery to create a composite.

Standard editorial layout adjustments — proportional resizing and minimal trim crops — are permitted, provided the integrity of the image is preserved.

---

## 6. Logos and Brandmarks

Logo and brandmark assets carry stricter rules:

- No modification of shape, proportion, colour or spacing.
- No application on backgrounds that compromise legibility.
- Minimum clear space around the mark equal to the height of the monogram.
- Light variants must be used on dark backgrounds and dark variants on light, as paired in the kit.
- Use only the Mirrou Gold (`#C6A56B`) version of the mark unless explicitly approved.

---

## 7. Geographic and Time Limits

These rights are granted globally and without expiration for the editorial, curatorial and partner uses defined in section 2. Mirrou reserves the right to request takedown of any specific use that violates this policy.

---

## 8. Persons in Images

Where individuals appear in imagery, model releases are on file with Mirrou. Recipients may use the imagery as released; recipients may not use the imagery in a manner that:

- Suggests endorsement by the depicted person of an unrelated product, service or political view.
- Removes the person's likeness from its original editorial context.
- Uses the person's image in a sensitive, satirical or defamatory manner without prior approval.

---

## 9. AI and Machine Learning

To remove ambiguity: Mirrou's assets are explicitly **not** licensed for use in:

- Training, fine-tuning or evaluating AI models.
- Generating synthetic imagery in the style of Mirrou or Olha Yevtushenko.
- Building datasets for resale or for distribution.

Any such use requires a separate written agreement.

---

## 10. Editorial Concept Frames

A small subset of frames in the Press Photo Kit is labelled `editorial-concept-...` in filenames and captions. These frames carry additional names (e.g. Aether, Neuroflow, Mininium) and are explicitly editorial concept frames produced by Mirrou Creative Studio to demonstrate visual range. They are **not** real products or third-party brands. When publishing these frames:

- Always include the caption "Editorial concept frame — not a real product or third-party brand."
- Do not present them as Mirrou client work or third-party brand work.

---

Mirrou will respond to any documented misuse with a takedown request via the recipient and, if necessary, via the publishing platform. Disputes may be addressed to `rights@mirrou.studio`.
