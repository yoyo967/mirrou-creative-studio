## LONG (≈ 180 words)

Olha Yevtushenko is the founder and creative director of Mirrou Creative Studio. She founded Mirrou as a response to a recurring problem in brand work: that beautiful campaigns rarely survive contact with the organisations that buy them. Mirrou was built to close that gap by treating image-making and creative governance as a single discipline.

Olha's work positions her at the intersection of personal artistic vision and studio-level performance — "the personal eye of a star photographer, scaled through the logic of a studio." At Mirrou, she leads the studio's signature lighting language, the Algorithm of Soul generative workflow, and the governance frameworks that make Mirrou's visual systems durable across campaigns and channels. The studio's work focuses on beauty, lifestyle and culture-led brands and is anchored in a single principle: aesthetics solve for performance.

Olha is based in Hamburg, with an additional studio presence in Berlin. Mirrou's conceptual case systems include Luminous Aura, Vitality Pulse, Essence Drift and Neural Glow.
