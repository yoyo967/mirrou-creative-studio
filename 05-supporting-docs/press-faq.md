# MIRROU — PRESS FAQ (v2)

Standard answers for editorial enquiries. Use verbatim or adapt with permission.

---

## 1. Can I use Mirrou's images for my article?

Yes — for editorial press use, with credit. The default credit line is `Photo: Olha Yevtushenko / Mirrou Creative Studio`. Logo and brandmark assets carry `© Olha Yevtushenko / Mirrou Creative Studio`. See `USAGE_RIGHTS.md` for the full policy.

## 2. Can I crop or recolour the images?

No, not without written approval from `rights@mirrou.studio`. Standard editorial layout adjustments — proportional resizing and minimal trim crops — are permitted; recolouring, retouching, mirroring, compositing and adding text are not.

## 3. Where do I get high-resolution material?

The Press Photo Kit (High-Res ZIP) on `mirrou.studio/press` contains the curated press selection. For high-resolution material beyond the public kit, write to `press@mirrou.studio`.

## 4. How should I refer to the studio and founder?

Refer to the studio as "Mirrou Creative Studio" on first mention; "Mirrou" thereafter. Capitalise as "Mirrou" — never "MIRROU" or "mirrou". Refer to the founder as "Olha Yevtushenko".

## 5. Where is the studio based?

Headquartered in Hamburg, with a second studio presence in Berlin. Studio visits by appointment only via `press@mirrou.studio`.

## 6. Can I interview the founder?

Yes — for editorial features, with at least 7 business days lead time. Send the request, outlet, format and angle to `press@mirrou.studio`.

## 7. Can I use the Mirrou logo / monogram?

Yes — for editorial coverage of Mirrou. Logos must not be modified in shape, proportion, colour or spacing. Light variants for dark backgrounds and dark variants for light backgrounds are paired in the kit.

## 8. Can I use Mirrou's images for AI training?

No. Mirrou's assets are explicitly not licensed for AI training, fine-tuning, dataset creation or generation of synthetic imagery in Mirrou's style. Such uses require a separate written agreement.

## 9. Can I use these images for commercial purposes?

No — the default licence covers editorial press, curatorial reference and partner internal use. Commercial use, advertising, sponsored content, packaging or product use require a separate written agreement from `rights@mirrou.studio`.

## 10. Are the cases — Luminous Aura, Vitality Pulse, Essence Drift, Neural Glow — real client brands?

No. These four cases are conceptual brand systems built by Mirrou Creative Studio to define and demonstrate visual language and method. They are presented as Mirrou's own editorial work.

## 11. There are other names in the imagery (e.g. Aether, Neuroflow, Mininium). Are those Mirrou cases too?

No. Frames carrying additional names are editorial concept frames used by Mirrou Creative Studio to demonstrate visual range. They are not real products or third-party brands. They are clearly marked in the press kit as "Editorial concept frame — not a real product or third-party brand."

## 12. Are the people in the photos consenting to editorial use?

Yes — model releases are on file with Mirrou for individuals appearing in the kit. Use must remain editorial; no use that suggests endorsement, removes the person from their original context, or is sensitive or defamatory.

## 13. Can I use the images on social media?

Yes — for editorial coverage that links to `mirrou.studio` or tags Mirrou directly. Do not use Mirrou's images as paid social or sponsored content for a third party.

## 14. What if my publication has a no-credit policy?

Then we cannot license the images for that use. Credit is required across all editorial channels.

## 15. Can I download the assets and host them on my own server?

For the duration of your editorial use, yes. Long-term redistribution, mirroring or making the assets publicly downloadable from your own infrastructure is not permitted.

## 16. How long does it take to get a response?

Press enquiries are answered within 2 business days. Interview requests typically require 7+ business days lead time.

## 17. Who do I contact?

- **Press:** press@mirrou.studio
- **Rights & usage:** rights@mirrou.studio
- **General:** hello@mirrou.studio
- **Languages:** EN, DE
