# HOW TO USE THE MIRROU PRESS PHOTO KIT (v2)

A one-page handout for journalists, curators and partners.

---

**1. Download.**
Get the Press Photo Kit from `mirrou.studio/press`. The ZIP contains ~60 curated high-resolution images plus credits, captions and a usage policy.

**2. Pick.**
Open the kit and choose the images that fit your story. The folders are organised by use case: `01_KEY-VISUALS/`, `02_PROJECTS/`, `03_STUDIO/`, `04_ABSTRACT/`, `05_EDITORIAL-CONCEPT/`.

**3. Caption.**
Open `CAPTIONS.csv`. Every file has a short and long caption ready to copy. Use them as-is or trim to fit your layout.

**4. Credit.**
- Photographs: `Photo: Olha Yevtushenko / Mirrou Creative Studio`
- Logos and brandmarks: `© Olha Yevtushenko / Mirrou Creative Studio`

**5. Don't modify.**
Standard layout adjustments — proportional resizing, minimal trim crops — are fine. Recolouring, retouching, mirroring, compositing and added text require written approval.

**6. Don't use commercially.**
The kit is licensed for editorial press use, curatorial reference and internal partner use. Advertising, sponsored content, packaging and product use require a separate agreement.

**7. Don't feed it to AI.**
The kit is explicitly not licensed for AI training, fine-tuning, dataset creation, or synthetic-image generation in Mirrou's or Olha Yevtushenko's style.

**8. Editorial concept frames.**
Frames inside `05_EDITORIAL-CONCEPT/` are concept frames produced by Mirrou — not real products or third-party brands. Always include the caption "Editorial concept frame — not a real product or third-party brand."

**9. The four cases — Luminous Aura, Vitality Pulse, Essence Drift, Neural Glow — are conceptual brand systems built by Mirrou Creative Studio**, not third-party client engagements. Treat them as Mirrou's own editorial work.

**10. Link.**
When publishing online, please link `Mirrou Creative Studio` to `mirrou.studio`.

**11. Need more?**
For high-resolution material beyond the kit, alternative formats, founder portraits, interviews or commercial permissions — write to `press@mirrou.studio` or `rights@mirrou.studio`. Reply within 2 business days.
